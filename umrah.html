Itinerary

Our thoughtfully crafted itinerary allows you to experience the essence of Umrah while ensuring ample time for spiritual reflection and devotion. Here's a glimpse of what you can expect

1. Arrival in Makkah

Begin your journey by arriving in the blessed city of Makkah. Complete your Ihram, the sacred state of pilgrimage.

3. Visit to Historical Sites

Explore significant sites in Makkah, including Jabal al-Nour, Jabal Thawr, and the Cave of Hira, gaining insights into the rich Islamic history.

5. Madinah Excursions

Take part in spiritually enriching excursions to Quba Mosque, Qiblatain Mosque, and the Uhud Mountain, where the Battle of Uhud took place.

2. Makkah Exploration

Engage in supplication, recitation of the Holy Quran, and reflect upon your personal connection with the Almighty at the holiest site of Islam.

4. Transfer to Madinah

Travel to the city of Madinah, the radiant city of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

6. Farewell to Madinah

Bid farewell to the serene city of Madinah, carrying the blessings and memories of your sacred journey.
